StockWatch
StockWatch is a website which shows stock market information in real time. It provides top ten losers and gainers of the day as observed in the stock market along with their other financial data, stock quotes, percentages and graphs. The data will have a maximum latency of 1 minute (due to caching since the website cannot put much load on the backend and use of free resources).
All financial data of the company including week highs and lows are listed as well as the company's metadata. Most graphs contain thousands of data point so that you can have a look the ticks at each point of time.
